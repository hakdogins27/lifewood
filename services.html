Our Data Solutions

We deliver complete, end-to-end data services engineered for superior accuracy, scalability, and efficiency.

Data Acquisition

We source and procure high-quality, diverse datasets tailored to your project's unique requirements. Leveraging a global network, we gather the essential raw materials—including text, images, audio, and video—needed to construct powerful and impartial AI models.

Data Collection

Our teams perform targeted data collection to meet specific criteria, ensuring you get the precise information required for your systems. This includes specialized media services like content classification, tagging, and generating broadcast subtitles.

Data Annotation

Precision is fundamental to machine learning. We offer meticulous, human-powered annotation and labeling for all data formats. Our strict quality control ensures high-fidelity ground truth, which is critical for training and refining elite AI algorithms.

Data Curation

We transform unstructured information into a valuable asset through systematic organization. Our curation process involves structuring, cleansing, and categorizing datasets to optimize them for seamless integration into your AI workflows.

Data Validation

Trust in your data is non-negotiable. We implement a rigorous validation framework to verify that your datasets are accurate, consistent, and conform to predefined standards, making them fit for any purpose.

Industry-Specific Applications

We leverage our core competencies to address complex, large-scale data challenges across various sectors.

Genealogy and Historical Archives

As a global leader in historical data processing, we convert fragile archival materials, handwritten ledgers, and census records into structured, searchable digital assets for the world's premier family history platforms.

Business Document Processing

Extract the full value from your company's documents. We deliver high-volume scanning and data extraction for invoices, claims, and other critical business forms with unparalleled accuracy and efficiency.

Digital Mailroom Automation

Streamline your entire mail management process. At our secure facilities, we handle the reception, sorting, and scanning of your incoming physical mail, delivering digitized information directly into your business applications.
