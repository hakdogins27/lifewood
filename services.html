Our Solutions

End-to-end data services designed for accuracy, scale, and speed.

Data Collection & Generation

We source and generate high-quality, diverse datasets tailored to your specific needs. From text and images to audio and video, our global network can acquire the raw materials required to build robust and unbiased AI models.

Data Labeling & Annotation

Accuracy is paramount. We provide precise, human-powered labeling and annotation services for all data types. Our rigorous quality assurance processes ensure high-fidelity ground truth data, essential for training and validating world-class machine learning algorithms.

Data Structuring & Cleansing

Transforming unstructured information into actionable insights requires meticulous organization. We structure, clean, validate, and categorize your datasets, ensuring they are optimized for efficient processing and analysis within your AI pipelines.

Specialized Data Solutions

We apply our core expertise to solve complex, large-scale data challenges for specific industries.

Genealogical & Historical Data

We are the global leader in processing historical documents. We convert handwritten records, census data, and delicate archival materials into structured, searchable digital assets for the world's leading family history platforms.

Document & Forms Processing

Unlock the value in your physical and digital documents. We provide high-volume scanning and data capture for invoices, claims, applications, and any other business-critical forms with industry-leading accuracy.

Digital Mailroom Services

Automate your entire mail workflow. We receive, open, sort, and scan your incoming physical mail at our secure facilities, delivering digitized documents and data directly into your business applications.
